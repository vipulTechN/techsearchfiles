about us

Search Homes India Pvt Ltd. was founded in 2015 and came into existence with the goal of providing one-to-one solutions for all your needs related to buying, selling, or renting properties.

We provide our customers with prompt and professional service. Our commitment to integrity, quality, and transparency are the foundation of our business and are reflected in everything we do. We believe that you will be pleased with our service and quality of work, as well as the value you receive for the cost.

We specialize in buying or selling of multiple residential and commercial properties like apartments, villas, plots, and farmlands which allows us to meet our clients' needs more precisely....

It all started with an understanding that buyers need a better way to search for their dream homes and properties, this realization has led to our founding principles today: simplicity and personal attention.


While buying your first home, feelings are running high. You are going from zero to something, and you need to begin somewhere, so you might be more disposed to neglect a couple of negatives. But, you are also going to have to live in that “somewhere,”. So it is basic to ensure that while you're not perspiring the little stuff, you are perceiving the genuinely significant things.

So, We use our expertise and unmatched network to find you the best home in India, whether you are looking for villa, plot apartment or an entire township project. We give importance to the customer's viewpoint and provide the best possible solution for their requirements.

1. We help you to shortlist the property that suits your requirement.
2. We arrange a site visit to all the short-listed properties.
3. We guide you on the price by keeping a tag on current market trends as well as a comparative analysis of the local market.
4. We don't stop at just finding your dream property but also assist you with procuring loans for the same.
5. We not only sell the property to customers from the trusted builders but also we provide the pre-sales and post-sales customer support to ensure our clients get the satisfaction from every point of view.
